SANNAS are the generative female counterpoints of the SANJAY collection. His equal (if not superior) in every way, SANNAS are 3,000 PFPs inspired by the female creators we respect most in the NFT space (and also: our mothers). SANNAS completes the SICKO Universe, and SANNAS pieces will be featured in the SICKO Universe comics.

A collaboration between SICKO lead artist, Alexander, and his mother—also an artist/illustrator—the collection features 7 female NFT artists who have submitted 1/1’s, backgrounds, Sandbox avatars, and other cool art!

SANNAS will mint at 0.03 ETH publicly on 03/31, preceded by a 48-72 hour WL mint window for SICKO holders, our collaborators’ communities, and a handful of additional projects’ collectors.

How did we start?

The original SANJAY project brought a lot of interesting people together in Discord, which had a very distinctive, intimate, and positive vibe. Our community has always been our most valuable asset, and as we endeavor to inform and attract new collectors and community members, we’re continously working on new projects to bring the most value for the community around SICKO Universe

About SANNAS

Our lead artist, Alexander, comes from a family of artists! And it was actually his mother (an artist too) who suggested that our OG SANJAYS weren’t much use to anybody without women in their life. Just as Adam needed Eve, our SANJAYS need SANNAS to thrive in the SICKO Universe. As such, the SANNAS collection is a family affair, and we look forward to introducing these female characters into our Universe. A 3,000-piece PFP collection with its particular roadmap, utility and artistic combination. We are damn proud of the SANNAS collection as we put the finishing touches on it. But, beyond the art, we’re absolutely hoping to be a positive part of the conversation on female builders (artists, devs, etc.) in the space and how we can help level the playing field.

As such, exploring the NFT space, we decided to give the SANNAS an immediate and tangible relevance to talented female creators in the NFT space, who often face more daunting challenges in building their communities and selling their pieces than male creators do.

So we reached out to several of the female NFT artists who we, as a team, geek out over, and we’re excited to announce collaborations with 7 female artists as part of the SANNAS collection! 1/1’s and/or generative layers, backgrounds, etc. will include works from our guest artists:

Lead SICKO artist, Alexander, comes by his talents naturally, as his artist/illustrator mother got him into drawing and painting at an early age, which is why their collaboration on SANNAS is such a poignant "full circle" moment!

His mother, Julia, studied art in college and went to work as an artist in a local theater as a young mother. The family did not have much money, which meant that Alexander too spent a lot of time at the theater, specifically attending art workshops and learning her trade firsthand.

Alexander worked on the corporate side of art for years until deciding to funnel his love of street art and illustration into the SICKO and SANJAY NFT collections in 2021. The rest, as they say, is history!
